* _2011_: I published and presented a paper at the _Theory and Practice of Computer Graphics 2011_ conference. The paper presented our https://castle-engine.io/compositing_shaders.php[Compositing Shaders, aka "shader effects"], which continue to be our advised way to use shaders in CGE.

* _2010_: I published and presented a paper at _Web3D 2010_ conference. I presented our X3D nodes to use https://castle-engine.io/x3d_extensions_shadow_maps.php[shadow maps], which continue to be how you can use _shadow maps_ in CGE.
